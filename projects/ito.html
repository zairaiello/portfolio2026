About Work

ITO

A corporate identity project focused on designing a fictional university from scratch.

CourseCorporate Identity Studio
TypeBranding
Year2025
RoleConcept, brand identity, poster, editorial layout, web design, merchandising items, presentations
TeamZaira Aiello, Martina Dolci, Silvia Panzeri, Lorenzo Oglialoro, Guilherme Ribeiro

The system establishes a complete visual and operational ecosystem, spanning from naming, logo, payoff and tone of voice to diverse applications. The brand identity unifies student stationery, postcards, banners, the website and retro gadgets, while fully coordinating the campus architecture, interior signage, academic course structures and hardware workshop collaborations.

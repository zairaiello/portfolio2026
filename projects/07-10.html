About Work

07/10

An editorial project focused on the concept of social indifference towards the Arab-Israeli conflict.

CourseVisual Design Fundamentals Studio
TypeEditorial
Year2024
RoleConcept, grid system, cover and back cover design, coordination
TeamZaira Aiello, Martina Dolci, Silvia Panzeri, Filippo Rondi

The volume analyses the visual and psychological aftermath of October 7th, weaving together historical research, political essays, maps, X posts and newspaper clippings. The editorial layout is defined by a high-contrast aesthetic — black-and-white photography, intense fire-red accents and raw halftone image processing — to reflect the dynamics of media saturation.

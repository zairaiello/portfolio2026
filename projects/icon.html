About Work

ICON.

A speculative project investigating symbolic conflict and the commodification of language.

CourseFinal Synthesis Studio C1
TypeSpeculative
Year2025
RoleConcept, brand identity, logo design, editorial layout, web design, data analysis, physical prototyping
TeamZaira Aiello, Gaia Cherubini, Martina Dolci, Silvia Panzeri, Martina Piccolo

Set in a plausible 2030 where global brands replace vocabulary, we established a fictional public authority to regulate this new marketplace. To bring this worldbuilding to life, we engineered a tangible "Starter Kit" using Arduino: a physical 4-slot syntax engine that instantly compiles custom-written, NFC chip-embedded brand tiles into legible digital sentences.

Steven Meisel

A monographic editorial project tracing the iconic career of the fashion photographer.

CourseHistory of Contemporary Art
TypeEditorial
Year2026
RoleTopic selection, text writing, concept, layout design, image curation
TeamZaira Aiello (individual project)

The volume features a curated retrospective of his most influential work for Vogue, exploring his artistic evolution from his early days to the present. Reflecting Meisel's sophisticated and provocative style, the publication adopts a minimalist, elegant layout, clean typography, balanced white space, and an uncompromising black-and-white aesthetic.
